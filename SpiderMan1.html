Spider-Man No Way Home Mod will add spider-man and his enemies to Minecraft Bedrock. Now you can feel like a real spider-man or become one of the villains who hunt him.

Installation:
— Download McPack
— Use the Minecraft
— Install McAddon or McPack files, just open it for this;
— Select new textures in the settings;
— Switch on the experimental mod;
— Done.
